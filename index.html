What's inside

One vault. Every everyday tool.

Each tool below runs entirely on your device — the line connecting them is the only network this app needs.

▣

Document scanner

camera → PDF

Point, capture, done — multi-page scans become clean PDFs saved straight to your Downloads. The camera feed is processed in memory and never transmitted.

⬚

PDF tools + offline OCR

merge · split · reorder · extract text

Merge, split, reorder, compress — and pull selectable text out of any PDF or photo with on-device OCR. Most OCR apps upload your documents to do this. This one can't: there's nothing to upload to.

◫

Image tools

compress · resize · convert

Hit any upload size limit with one tap — smart compression binary-searches quality to land under your target. HEIC, WebP, PNG, JPEG, converted locally in seconds.

▤

Bill Vault

receipt → merchant · date · amount

Snap or pick a receipt and it's read on-device — merchant, date, total and GSTIN pulled out automatically, then filed in a private vault you can search and total up. Most expense apps ship your receipts to a server to read them. This one never sends a single byte.

⇄

Local share

phone → any browser, same Wi-Fi

Send a file to any laptop or phone on your network: the app spins up a tiny server on your device, the receiver scans a QR. The file travels across the room — not across the internet.

●

Voice notes & tasks

speak → searchable text

Dictate notes and voice tasks with reminders, transcribed as you speak and stored in a local database. Search everything later — your voice stays yours.

▦

QR studio + file manager

generate · scan · organize

Generate and scan QR codes, and manage every file the vault produces — rename, move, share, or extract text, all from one place.

✦

App lock & Vault Pulse

PIN · biometric · activity constellation

A PIN and biometric gate guards the whole vault, with brute-force lockout. Vault Pulse turns your real activity into a living constellation — drawn from local data, like everything else here.
